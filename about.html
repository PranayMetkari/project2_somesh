Welcome Estonia Handloom

At Estonia Handloom, we don’t just produce textiles — we weave stories. Stories of skill, tradition, patience, and the quiet resilience of artisan hands that have passed down their craft through generations.

Founded with a mission to revive traditional handloom artistry and bring it into the modern global marketplace, Estonia Handloom represents a harmonious blend of heritage and innovation. Our name reflects a commitment to authenticity, sustainability, and handcrafted quality, where each product holds meaning beyond material.

We work directly with weaver communities across India and other craft-rich regions to ensure that every product is handcrafted, ethically made, and environmentally responsible. From natural fibres to non-toxic dyes, and from ancient techniques to modern designs, our process is a seamless flow of culture meeting creativity.

Whether it's a delicate cotton saree, a handwoven linen curtain, or a bespoke textile collection for a brand, we serve clients who value craftsmanship over mass production and character over conformity.

why Choose Us

Our products are handwoven on traditional looms — no power looms, no digital shortcuts, no mass production

We use natural fibres, zero-plastic packaging, eco dyes, and no-waste policies throughout our supply chain.

We work directly with artisan groups — ensuring fair pay, community support, and no middlemen exploitation

Each project is approached with detail, flexibility, and collaboration — from material choice to final design.

From sampling to shipping, we’re equipped for international clients, with a team that understands both design and logistics.
