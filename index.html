Welcome Estonia Handloom

At Estonia Handloom, we don’t just produce textiles — we weave stories. Stories of skill, tradition, patience, and the quiet resilience of artisan hands that have passed down their craft through generations.

Founded with a mission to revive traditional handloom artistry and bring it into the modern global marketplace, Estonia Handloom represents a harmonious blend of heritage and innovation. Our name reflects a commitment to authenticity, sustainability, and handcrafted quality, where each product holds meaning beyond material.

Why Choose Estonia Handloom?

Authenticity Over Automation

Our products are handwoven on traditional looms — no power looms, no digital shortcuts, no mass production.

Sustainability at the Core

We use natural fibres, zero-plastic packaging, eco dyes, and no-waste policies throughout our supply chain.

Transparency & Fair Trade

We work directly with artisan groups — ensuring fair pay, community support, and no middlemen exploitation.

Custom Craftsmanship

Each project is approached with detail, flexibility, and collaboration — from material choice to final design.

Global-Ready Operations

From sampling to shipping, we’re equipped for international clients, with a team that understands both design and logistics.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Yes, every product at Estonia Handloom is genuinely handcrafted by skilled artisans using traditional loom techniques. We do not use power looms or automated production processes — only pure, handwoven artistry.

We use natural and sustainable materials such as cotton, linen, silk, wool, and jute. Our yarns are responsibly sourced, and we prefer vegetable-based or azo-free dyes to ensure eco-conscious production.

Yes, we ship worldwide. We ensure careful packaging and reliable logistics support to deliver our products safely to your doorstep, wherever you are.

Absolutely! We offer custom handloom services for brands, designers, and individuals. You can choose patterns, colors, fabric types, and sizing. Just drop us a brief or email, and our team will guide you through the process.

Yes, we welcome B2B inquiries. Whether you're a boutique, lifestyle brand, or interior designer, we can fulfill bulk or private-label orders with consistent quality, export documentation, and reliable turnaround times.

Ready-to-ship items: 5–10 business days (India) / 10–14 days (International)
Made-to-order/custom: 3–4 weeks depending on complexity and quantity.
We’ll share a tracking link once your product is dispatched.

Handloom fabrics are delicate and best preserved with care:
  • Prefer cold water, mild detergent, and gentle hand-wash
  • Avoid machine drying
  • Iron on low heat
  • Store in a dry place away from direct sunlight

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, bank transfers, and secure international gateways like Stripe or PayPal (for overseas orders).

Yes, we’re always open to meaningful collaborations with ethical brands, NGOs, or designers interested in handloom development, capacity building, or co-creating collections.

You can email us at info@estoniahandloom.com, fill out the contact form on our website, or DM us on Instagram @EstoniaHandloom. Our team usually responds within 24–48 hours.
